Rooted in the belief that real English education should not be a privilege.

Masters of Real English is a nonprofit organization dedicated to closing the language arts gap for students who need it most. We offer rigorous coursework — honors prep, AP English, and SAT verbal — at accessible price points, grounded in the conviction that high-quality instruction should be the rule, not the exception.

Our approach is substance over shortcuts: we teach students to truly think, write, and communicate — skills that outlast any single exam.

What We Offer

Three pathways.
One standard of excellence.

Each program is crafted for a distinct academic milestone, all unified by our commitment to deep learning over test-taking tricks.

Foundations

Honors Class Prep

Build the reading comprehension, analytical writing, and literary vocabulary needed to thrive in an honors-level English course.

  • Close reading techniques
  • Analytical paragraph structure
  • Vocabulary in context
  • Literary device mastery
Advanced

AP English Prep

Prepare for AP Literature and/or AP Language with focused essay training, rhetorical analysis, and timed writing under exam conditions.

  • Free response essay drills
  • Rhetorical analysis practice
  • Argument synthesis writing
  • AP exam scoring rubric training
Test Prep

SAT Verbal Prep

Target the Reading and Writing sections of the Digital SAT with adaptive practice, evidence-based strategies, and score-milestone tracking.

  • Digital SAT format mastery
  • Evidence-based reading skills
  • Grammar & usage patterns
  • Score improvement tracking
